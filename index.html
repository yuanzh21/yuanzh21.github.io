Short Biography

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. Candidate in Engineering Sciences (Mechanical Engineering) at University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Jorge Cortés. During my Ph.D., I also work closely with Prof. Yuanyuan Shi (UCSD), Dr. Guido Cavraro (NREL), and Prof. Changhong Zhao (CUHK). Prior to that, I was enrolled in the successive undergraduate and postgraduate program at the Honors School of Harbin Institute of Technology, where I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Control Science and Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Wu.
